Party in BA

Palermo -with a huge variety- is the go-to area for bars, clubs and parties. If you wish to have a few drinks or brews with you friends, pay attention to the closing hours which are often at 4 a.m. If you prefer to go dancing, there are options to keep you awake until sunrise. Many other neighborhoods have a vibrant nightlife, check all your options before deciding!
